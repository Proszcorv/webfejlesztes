THE MID LANE

Mid laners are your high burst damage champions who can do it all—solo and as a team. For them, combat is a dangerous dance where they're always looking for an opportunity to outplay their opponent. One of the most important task for a midlaner is to roam

ROAMING

Roaming is essentially the act of leaving your own lane to assist teammates in other parts of the map. When executed properly, it's a skill that can apply pressure, create opportunities for your team, and disrupt the enemy's plans. Understanding how to roam in League of Legends is crucial for players who want to make a significant impact on the game's outcome.
